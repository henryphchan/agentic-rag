StellarTech, a prominent software company based in Berlin, recently acquired QuantumCorp for $500 million. QuantumCorp specializes in quantum cryptography and was founded by Dr. Aris Thorne in 2018. Following the acquisition, Dr. Aris Thorne was appointed as the Chief Scientist of StellarTech. He is currently leading a highly classified initiative called Project Obsidian. Project Obsidian utilizes the Oracle Cloud Infrastructure to process massive cryptographic datasets securely.

The primary competitor to Project Obsidian is the Aurora Framework. The Aurora Framework was developed by Nexus Industries, a secretive cybersecurity firm headquartered in Tokyo. Nexus Industries is currently managed by CEO Elena Rostova. To gain a competitive edge, Nexus Industries is actively trying to recruit former engineers from QuantumCorp.

To bolster their infrastructure against Nexus Industries, StellarTech signed a strategic partnership with Qdrant Cloud. Qdrant Cloud will provide the advanced vector database capabilities necessary for Project Obsidian's semantic search and retrieval-augmented generation features. This architecture ensures that Dr. Aris Thorne can deploy localized language models securely.
